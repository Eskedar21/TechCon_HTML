History

"TechCon, initially launched in 2005 by tech enthusiasts in Ethiopia, has grown into a prestigious annual event that garners global attention. Featuring forward-thinking discussions, groundbreaking presentations, and impactful networking opportunities, the event continues to drive tech innovation in Africa and beyond."

Mission

At TechCon 2024, the goal is to drive innovation and collaboration in the technology sector by bringing together industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts from around the world. The focus is on fostering an environment of learning, inspiration, and connection to empower participants to push the boundaries of what's possible. This is achieved through insightful presentations, hands-on workshops, and engaging discussions, sparking creativity and facilitating the exchange of groundbreaking ideas to transform the future of technology.
